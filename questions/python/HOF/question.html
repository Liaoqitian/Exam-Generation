Given the following:
a. map
b. keep
c. combine
d. A dyadic reporter named F
e. A predicate named P
f. A list named L

1. Of the 6 possible ways of nesting items 1-3 above (e.g., map(keep(combine)) or keep(combine(map))), how many can error?
(Remember calling map or keep on an empty list does not error.)


2. How many can give an output other than error?


3. Give a concrete example specifying items F, P, and L such that combine(map(keep)) and combine(keep(map)) output the same results, but not error.
